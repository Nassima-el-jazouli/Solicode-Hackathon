The most pressing problem is that 55% of fresh dairy product waste comes from consumers. Therefore, reducing the waste of dairy products requires not only initiatives on the production side, but also innovative solutions to help consumers reduce their waste. This is where we came with our digital solution EcoCycle.

Packaging recycling belongs to a specific recycling channel, just like car recycling, oil recycling, clothing recycling and pallet recovery. A packaging is a set of materials intended to contain, identify, protect, preserve and promote a product from its distribution to its storage. In France, the recycling rate for packaging bearing the recycling symbol is around 44%, including household waste and industrial waste.
